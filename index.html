St. Paul’s English High School!

St. Paul’s English High School has been in existence for the last 71 years. Established by the Catholic Church in Karachi, to impart a holistic education to the students, of various creeds, that come to us to learn. The electronic media is fast expanding and is easily accessible. We have our ex-students spread all over the world, who, I am sure, would love to know what is happening in their Alma Mater, the Paulian Family. For this purpose, we have decided to have an official website of St. Paul’s English High School, to keep you updated with all that is happening in the school. We will endeavor to update you on all that is going on in the school regarding admissions, co-curricular activities Matric and Cambridge results, sports etc.

About Us

Welcome to St Paul's English High School

The vision of our school is to animate and guide the process for the full development of the personality of a child so that he / she becomes a good, self-reliant, self-disciplined and useful citizen so that he / she could honestly and conscientiously face the challenges of life and influence and serve society for the common good.

To work by example for the all-round development of the personality of the child through value-based, conceptual and research-oriented education and through the development of skills needed to be a useful, a helpful and a good citizen of the country and of the world for the glory of God and service to humanity.
